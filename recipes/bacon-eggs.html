Cookin Up Bacon and Eggs

Brakfast for a Beast

A classic and easy brakfast meal to enjoy at anytime

Ingredients

  • 6 eggs
  • 1/4lb of Premium Bacon

Steps

  1. Dice bacon
  2. Put a pan on stove top at medium high heat
  3. When pan is hot, place bacon into pan
  4. Stir bacon until brown and crispy to your liking
  5. Crack the 6 eggs into a bowl and toss the shells into trash
  6. Season eggs with salt and pepper then scramble
  7. Pour eggs into pan with bacon
  8. Stir bacon and eggs until nice and fluffy
  9. Take off heat, serve and enjoy
