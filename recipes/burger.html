How To Make The Krabby Patty

Burger of the Gods

The Greatest burger in all of the seven seas.

Ingredients

  • Krabby Patty Buns
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Pickles
  • Onions
  • Cheese
  • Krabby Patty
  • Secret Formula

Steps

  1. Put Krabby Patty on the grill
  2. Cook for 5 minutes and flip
  3. Toast buns on the grill to desired toastiness
  4. Set Down Bottom bun and place Krabby Patty
  5. Starting with the Cheese, place your toppings. Lettuce, Tomato, Onions, and Pickles
  6. Enjoy The best burger under the seven seas
